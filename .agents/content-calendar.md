## Volume Framework

**Core rule: 2 posts/week per active sport + 1 evergreen post/week (minimum).**

Not every post is a 30-minute carousel. The volume increase comes from quick-hit content:
- **X-only tweets** (hot takes, replies to news, quick screenshots) — ~5 min each
- **Cross-platform posts** (IG carousel + X + FB) — ~20 min each
- **IG Stories** (polls, behind-the-build, quick reactions) — ~3 min each

A "7 posts/week" week might actually look like: 3 quick X tweets + 2 cross-platform posts + 2 IG Stories = ~90 min total creation + ~75 min engagement = **under 3 hours.**

**Active sport count drives volume:**

| Active Sports | Min Posts/wk | Example Months |
|---|---|---|
| 2 sports | 5 | Feb (🏀🏒), Jun–Jul (⚾ + 🏈 ramp) |
| 3 sports | 7 | Jan, Mar, May, Nov, Dec |
| 4 sports | 8–10 | Apr, Oct (all four live) |
| PEAK (draft/opening) | 10+ | Aug–Sep (🏈 draft + ⚾ playoffs) |

**Sport allocation per post (when multiple sports are active):**
Give the primary sport ~50% of posts, secondary sports ~20–25% each, and save 1 post/week for evergreen culture/founder content that isn't sport-specific.

## Content Pillars (quick reference)

| # | Pillar | What it is | Best timing |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **League Culture** | The social experience of being in a league. Types of league mates, commissioner life, group chat dynamics, trophies, rivalries. | Evergreen — use more in off-season when topical content is thin |
| 2 | **Analytical Wedge** | Product proof. Screenshots of Power Rankings, Win Probability, Draft Grades, H2H. "Here's what your league actually looks like." | In-season — needs active league data to show |
| 3 | **League Archaeology** | History, all-time records, Tale of the Tape, "your league has stats from 2017 you've never seen." | Off-season + end-of-season — perfect for "look back" moments |
| 4 | **Founder POV** | Tuesday night origin, behind-the-build, what I'm learning, why I built this. | Evergreen — use more in off-season, 1/week max |
| 5 | **Topical / Cultural** | Reacting to sports news, uniform reveals, trades, awards, schedule releases — through a fantasy-league lens. | Requires active news cycle — heaviest in-season |

## Monthly Planning Ritual (do this on the 1st of each month)

1. Open this document. Read the current month's section.
2. Note the **key moments** — put them on your calendar.
3. Look at the **pillar mix** — plan your posts around it.
4. Check **which FB groups** to focus engagement in.
5. Set the **paid ad budget** for the month.
6. Batch the first 2 weeks of content on the first Sunday.
7. Leave room for reactive/topical content — don't over-schedule.
8. At month end: review what worked, adjust next month's plan.
